**Project:**
A Matter of Taste: This lab explores the relationship between smell and taste. I conducted an experiment to see if pinching someone's nose actually changes their ability to identify different soda flavors.

Project Details:
- **Experiment:** 20 total trials (10 with nose pinched, 10 without).
- **Analysis:** Used a **Permutation Test** via the `infer` package in R to analyze the results.
- **Findings:** The experiment resulted in a **p-value of 0.29**. 
- **Summary:** Because the p-value is quite high, we don't have enough evidence to say that pinching the nose makes a significant difference in identifying the flavors for this specific test.
